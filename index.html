Our Story

Since 1981 Petrick Lock & Safe has been providing for the security needs of the Kitsap and Olympic peninsula. In addition to simple home and automotive lock-outs, we offer professional safe openings, Victorian era lock repair and replacement, high security systems, and facility-wide rekeys. From a simple house rekey to opening a high-security safe, give us a call and we'll help you through your insecurities.
