Our Story

Since 1981 Petrick Lock & Safe has been providing for the security needs of the Kitsap and Olympic peninsula. In addition to simple home and automotive lock-outs, we offer professional safe openings, Victorian era lock repair and replacement, high security systems, and facility-wide rekeys. We also pride ourselves on being a family of locksmiths, with my daughter Anya taking over as the next generation of Petricks working to enhance the state of home security. From a simple house rekey to opening a high-security safe, don't hesitate to give us a call.
